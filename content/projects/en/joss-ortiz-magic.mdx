## Overview

Joss Ortiz is a talented illusionist who needed a platform that matched his art. Despite his technical skill, he lacked a consistent visual identity and an official channel where his audience could find and hire him.

## The Challenge

The challenge was to encapsulate the essence of "modern magic" into a digital identity that was both professional and mysterious, functional and artistic. We needed to build everything from scratch: logo, color palette, website, and social channels.

## The Solution

We built a robust digital ecosystem designed to impress:
- **Branding:** We developed a visual identity based on the concept of "invisible details" that make magic possible.
- **Web Platform:** A high-performance site optimized with Astro to reflect elegance and speed.
- **Audiovisual Production:** We created content that captures the emotion of his acts, designed specifically for social media.

Joss now has a solid digital presence that not only showcases his talent but also facilitates the acquisition of corporate and private clients.
